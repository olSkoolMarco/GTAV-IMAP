How to submit new locations



If you would like to add a new location/locations to our map, make sure to read the following guidelines.
And submit a request via the form below:


Submission Guidelines:

-Make sure that the listing you are submitting isn’t already on our map.
-Submission must have a valid text tutorial link.
- If you don’t know who the founder of glitch is, please do a bit of a back ground check and for example, add earliest YouTube or Se7enSins date that it was documented.
(We will continue to do background searches on breaches and re-adjust if required. If it was found that you have done so, to claim credit. Submission will be removed or not be approved)
- Video tutorial quality: The video tutorial must clearly show method that is used, to breach into location.
- Breaches that can be done anywhere, for example like with using a tank. Can be rejected for submission. As we do not want this map to be full of these type breaches (but we will try to be as lenient as possible).


Also please Click here to view our sites terms and conditions.
*Please note by submitting this form below, you agree to our terms and conditions.




How to get coordinates for your location.
To find the actual coordinates for your new breach location, you can use the coordinate/debug mode on our map:

- Click here to open map in a new window.
- Right click on that location, to create a marker. Marker will show you the actual coordinates (latitude and longitude) for your location.
- You can also move the marker anywhere on map, to change location. ( The coordinates will update while you drag ).


Review and approval period.
Once you sent us over the new location you want to add, you'll need to be patient. As it may take some time before your location is approved. This depends mainly on it’s position in the queue, though will usually be no more than 3 days.
If for some reason your request does not get approved. You will be notified via email, which will also state reason for request not being approved.
